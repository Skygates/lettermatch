LetterMatch is being built by people who are tired of the algorithm deciding what love looks like.

Why we're building this

Modern dating apps are extraordinary feats of engineering — built to keep you engaged, scrolling, and paying, not to help you fall in love. They've turned human connection into a marketplace, and most of us have felt what that costs.

We've matched with hundreds. Met nobody. Or met someone, rushed past the part where you actually get to know them, and watched it collapse before it started. The problem isn't you. It's the design.

"The art of letter writing is the art of revealing yourself through language — slowly, deliberately, honestly."

LetterMatch is our attempt to rebuild dating around something slower. The idea is simple: what if the first thing you learned about someone was who they are, not what they look like?

Letters. A 4-hour delivery window that makes you think before you write. A rule that you can never ghost — you must say a real goodbye if you choose to leave. These aren't gimmicks. They're design decisions built around a belief: that depth is worth the wait.

What we're not

We are not a finished product. As of March 2026, LetterMatch is in active development. There is no app yet. There is no matching algorithm yet. There are no letter exchanges yet.

What exists is the idea, a small and passionate team, and a growing waitlist of people who feel the same way we do. We are building in public because we believe the people who join us before we launch deserve to know exactly where we are.

A note on the waitlist: Joining now does not buy access, priority, or special treatment. It tells us this should exist. We will email you when we have something real to share and when we are ready to invite people in.

Our principles

Slowness is a feature. Everything on LetterMatch is intentionally unhurried. Letters take 4 hours to arrive. You write with intention, not impulse.

Words over appearance. You read a person's letter and their poem before you see their face. Connection begins with mind and voice.

No ghosting. You must write a closing letter if you choose to stop. Because every person deserves an honest ending, not silence.

No AI-written letters. We use detection to ensure every word is human. This isn't a shortcut. It's a promise.

Your data is yours. We do not sell your data, target you with advertising, or build profiles to monetize. Read our Privacy Policy for the full picture.
