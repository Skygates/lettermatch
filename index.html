Fall in love with a mind before a face.

Every step is designed to slow you down. The people worth finding are worth the wait.

01
✍️

Write your first letter

Not a bio. A real letter — at least 100 words about who you actually are. You choose one poem from our library that speaks for you when words fail.

02
📖

Someone reads your words

People browse your letter and your poem — not just your face. If something moves in them, they write back. That's how it begins.

03
⏳

Letters take 4 hours to arrive

Always. No exceptions. Time to think about what you want to say. Time to actually mean it. Anticipation is part of falling in love.

04
🤝

No ghosting. Ever.

To stop writing, you must write a closing letter. A real, honest goodbye — because every person deserves an ending that means something.

05
💌

Up to 10 rounds of letters

The conversation deepens across up to 10 exchanges. AI detection ensures every word is human. No shortcuts. No copy-paste. Just you.

06
☕

When you're both ready — you meet

Two people who already know each other sit face to face for the first time. That meeting isn't awkward. It feels like coming home.
