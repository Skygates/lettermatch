Privacy Policy

We built LetterMatch because we believe your data belongs to you — and this policy explains exactly what we do with it.

Last updated: March 2026

Plain English summary: We collect your email and your answer to our question. We use this only to contact you about LetterMatch. We do not sell your data. We do not advertise to you. When the product launches, you'll be able to delete your account and all associated data at any time. This policy describes our current data practices during the pre-launch phase.

1. Who we are

LetterMatch ("we," "us," "our") is a pre-launch project building a letter-based dating platform. We are not yet a registered corporation. This website is operated by the founding team. Contact us at privacy@lettermatch.com for any privacy-related matters.

2. What data we collect

During the current pre-launch phase, we collect:

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or make a payment
  • Your answer to our single waitlist question
  • Timestamp of your submission
  • Payment details — processed entirely by PayPal. We never see or store your card number, bank details, or full payment credentials. We only receive confirmation of a completed payment and the amount.
  • First name — if provided voluntarily or through PayPal's confirmation

We do not collect: your location, your device information, browsing history, or any biometric data. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising networks.

3. Why we collect it

  • Email: To notify you when LetterMatch launches or when we have meaningful updates to share.
  • Your answer: To read and understand who is joining, and to demonstrate to you that a real person reads what you write.
  • Payment: To record that a Founding Member contribution was made and to fulfil the benefits associated with it when the product launches.

We will never use your email to send marketing for other products, third-party offers, or anything unrelated to LetterMatch.

4. How we store it

Waitlist data is stored in Google Sheets via Google's infrastructure (Google Workspace, subject to Google's privacy and security standards). Payment confirmations are logged with minimal identifying information. All data is accessible only to the founding team.

We are in a pre-launch phase and do not yet operate production-grade infrastructure. When we launch, we will migrate to a secure, purpose-built database and update this policy accordingly.

5. Who we share data with

We share your data with no one for marketing or commercial purposes. The only third parties who may process your data as part of our infrastructure are:

  • Google (Workspace/Sheets) — stores waitlist submissions
  • PayPal — processes payments; we do not share your data with PayPal; you submit it directly to them

We will never sell, rent, or license your personal data to any third party.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (we will action this within 30 days)
  • Withdraw consent to communications at any time by emailing privacy@lettermatch.com or unsubscribing from any email we send

Depending on your country of residence, additional rights may apply under the GDPR (EU/EEA), UK GDPR, CCPA (California), or other applicable law. Contact us to exercise any rights.

7. Payments & refunds

All payments are processed by PayPal. We do not store payment card information. PayPal's Privacy Policy governs the data you submit to them during checkout.

As described in our Terms of Service, contributions to LetterMatch at this stage are voluntary acts of support. Because this is a pre-launch project with no guaranteed delivery date, contributions are generally non-refundable. If you have a concern about a payment, contact us at support@lettermatch.com and we will address it honestly.

8. Cookies

This website does not set cookies for analytics, tracking, or advertising. The only cookies that may be set are those required by PayPal's payment SDK for secure transaction processing. These are strictly necessary and are not used to track you across other websites.

9. Children

LetterMatch is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact privacy@lettermatch.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices evolve — particularly when the product launches and we operate a full user database. We will notify waitlist members of material changes by email before they take effect.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: privacy@lettermatch.com
