Privacy Policy

We built LetterMatch because we believe your data belongs to you, and this policy explains exactly what we do with it.

Last updated: March 2026

Plain English summary: We collect your email address, your answer to one question, and the time you submitted it. We use that information only to manage the waitlist and contact you about LetterMatch. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads against it.

1. Who we are

LetterMatch ("we," "us," "our") is a pre-launch project building a letter-based dating platform. We are not yet a registered corporation. This website is operated by the founding team. Contact us at privacy@lettermatch.com for privacy-related matters.

2. What data we collect

During the current pre-launch phase, we collect:

  • Email address when you join the waitlist
  • Your answer to our waitlist question
  • Timestamp of your submission
  • Any information you send us directly by email if you contact us

We do not intentionally collect billing details, advertising identifiers, or profile data beyond what you choose to submit.

3. Why we collect it

  • Email: To notify you when LetterMatch launches or when we have meaningful updates to share.
  • Your answer: To understand who is joining, what they care about, and to keep the waitlist human rather than anonymous.
  • Direct emails: To respond to questions, support requests, or other messages you send us.

We will never use your email to send third-party marketing or unrelated offers.

4. How we store it

Waitlist data is stored in Google Sheets through Google Workspace. Any direct email correspondence is stored in the inboxes used by the founding team. Access is limited to the people operating LetterMatch.

We are still in a pre-launch phase and do not yet operate production-grade application infrastructure. When that changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

5. Who we share data with

We do not share your data with anyone for marketing or commercial purposes. The third parties that may process your data as part of our infrastructure are:

  • Google Workspace / Google Sheets for storing waitlist submissions
  • Email providers used by the founding team when receiving or sending direct email

We will never sell, rent, or license your personal data to any third party.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to communications at any time by emailing privacy@lettermatch.com or unsubscribing from any email we send

Depending on your country of residence, additional rights may apply under the GDPR (EU/EEA), UK GDPR, CCPA (California), or other applicable law. Contact us to exercise any rights.

7. Cookies

This website does not intentionally set cookies for analytics, tracking, advertising, or checkout flows. Standard browser caching or infrastructure-level technical logging may still occur as part of normal web delivery, but we do not use those mechanisms for ad targeting.

8. Children

LetterMatch is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact privacy@lettermatch.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices evolve, particularly when the product launches and we operate a full user database. We will notify waitlist members of material changes by email before they take effect.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: privacy@lettermatch.com
